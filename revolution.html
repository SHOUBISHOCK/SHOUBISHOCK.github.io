About Us

Revolution is a brand new mod for Insurgency (from 2014, NOT Insurgency: Sandstorm) that gives the game a third-person perspective and attempts to provide a SOCOM-like experience.

Tollski made the mod release, LupinWulf added a sandbox-ish side to it!

With features like bullet time, firesupport, dismemberment and so much more without unbalance things!
